Santa Barbara

Here are some random facts about where I live!

Along the Pacific Coast, Santa Barbara serenely sits just 92 miles (148 kilometers) north of Los Angeles and 332 miles (534 kilometers) south of San Francisco.

Approximately 90,412 residents reside within Santa Barbara city limits. 435,697 reside in all of Santa Barbara County.

Santa Barbara is in the Pacific Time Zone, observing daylight savings times from mid-March to early November.

The weather is moderate year-round Mediterranean climate 15-20 degrees C/60-80 degrees.

Twenty-five beaches stretch approximately 100 miles/160 kilometers of south-facing, scenic shoreline.
